Movemaster commands:

Motion and positioning:
MJ - move joint
MP - move position
SP - speed

ED - end of command function

MJ [<waist joint angle>], [<shoulder joint angle>],
[<elbow joint angle>], [<pitch joint angle>], [<roll joint
angle>] min angle = 0.01

WH response +-1,+-1,+-1,+-1,+-1, +-1, R,A,N,O
